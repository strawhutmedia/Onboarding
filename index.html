      Onboarding Checklist for

      Your form has been submitted. Here's what happens next.

      Phase 1

      Kickoff

      • Schedule Brainstorm Meeting

        Our first call to discuss your vision, format, and goals for the show.

      • Complete Brainstorm Meeting

        Align on podcast concept, format, recording approach, and timeline.

      Phase 2

      Branding & Design

      • Finalize Branding Guidelines

        Establish colors, typography, voice, and visual identity for the show.

      • Create Show Logo

        Design the primary logo for the podcast.

      • Create Podcast Cover Art

        3000x3000px artwork for Apple Podcasts, Spotify, etc.

      • Create YouTube Channel Art

        Banner, profile image, and video-format cover art for YouTube.

      • Create Episode Thumbnail Templates

        Reusable templates for per-episode thumbnails on YouTube.

      • Create End Screen Graphics

        Ending cards/graphics for YouTube and Spotify video episodes.

      • Create Social Media Promo Graphics

        Templates for promoting episodes on Instagram, X, LinkedIn, etc.

      • Create Transition / Segment Graphics

        Motion graphics for segment transitions within full episodes.

      Phase 3

      Production

      • Confirm Recording Setup

        Finalize location, equipment, and technical setup for recording.

      • First Recording Session

        Record the first episode (or pilot) of the show.

      • Post-Production & Editing

        Edit audio/video, add graphics, transitions, and finalize the episode.

      • Distribution & Platform Setup

        Set up podcast hosting, submit to directories, configure YouTube channel.

      • Publish First Episode

        Launch the first episode across all platforms.
